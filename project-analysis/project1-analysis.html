Monopoly Analysis

Completed: April 2024 Tech Stack: Python, Tableau

Project Overview

I wanted to do this project after I lost a game to my 7 year old daughter and I do not want to ever lose another game. I set out to answer three questions about the game of Monopoly. I play way to much of this game on my phone and with board game to ever lose to my child again.

The Challenge

Description of the problem you were trying to solve.

  • What is the most valuable color group
  • What is the least valuable color group
  • What is the most efficient strategy for winning

The Solution

I found a dataset that had 60,000 rolls of the dice from actual Monopoly games. This dataset allowed me to analyze the game mechanics in depth.

Key Features

  • Orange Properties are the most landed on color group.
  • The Utilities are the least landed on as well as the dark blue properties.
  • Buying the orange properties and the red properties is the best strategy for winning.

Lessons Learned

I learned how to become a better player at monopoly by understanding the game data and strategies.
